About This App
RemotexNote is an applicaon that allows you to record your heart and lung sounds and take related
notes.
Your health is important, especially when it comes to your health notes.
Did you know you can record heart and lung sounds using your phone's microphone? RemotexNote
records your heart and lung sounds using your phone's microphone. You can take notes for instant
recordings, such as "before workout" or "aer workout." You can share the recorded heart and lung
sounds with your loved ones and use the notes as reminder references.
RemotexNote enhances and strengthens the recorded sounds using various algorithms. This allows
you to listen to your heart and lung sounds.
Using the app is very simple. Place the microphone secon at the boom of your phone against your
skin and keep it steady during recording. Record for 10 seconds and review your recordings.
What can RemotexNote do?
-Records your heart and lung sounds from the skin surface.
-Allows you to take notes related to the recorded sounds.
-Enables you to review the recorded notes later.
-Allows you to share the recorded heart and lung sounds with your loved ones.
